“以形为治” 源流及其药用体系的初步构建
刘庆华，贾春华*
(北京中医药大学中医学院，北京市朝阳区北三环东路11号，100029)
［摘要］ “以形为治”由徐灵胎提出，是对部位用药的深度概括。在回顾 “以形为治”源流的基础上，探
讨 “以形为治”的思想理论基础，并从隐喻认知的立场对枸杞、紫苏、桑、莲四种药物的用药思维模式进
行讨论，并将 “以形为治”的部位对应关系总结为本草之根多对应人体脏腑、本草之皮多对应人体体表、
本草梗茎多对应人体脏腑中的胃肠、本草之枝干多对应人体四肢、本草苗叶和花多对应人体体表、本草果实
和种子多对应人体目与生殖系统。以此论证了古人 “皮以治皮，节以治骨，核以治丸，子能明目，蔓藤舒
筋脉，枝条达四肢”用药认知的合理性，并初步构建 “以形为治”药用体系。

Discussion on Theoretical Sources of“Therapeutic Effects of Herbs Based on Shapes”and the Preliminary
Construction of Its Medicinal System
LIU Qinghua，JIA Chunhua
(School of Traditional Chinese Medicine，Beijing University of Chinese Medicine，Beijing100029)
ABSTＲACT “Therapeutic effects of herbs based on shapes”is proposed by XU Lingtai，which is a summary of
the medication used by the parts． On the basis of reviewing the theoretical sources of“therapeutic effects of herbs
based on shapes”，this paper explored the theoretical basis of“therapeutic effects of herbs based on shapes”，and the
application mindset of four Chinese herbs: medlar，perilla，mulberry and lotus was discussed from the view of meta-
phorical cognition． In the discussion，the following corresponding relationships were summarized，i. e. the roots of
the Chinese herbs are mostly applied to the organs of human body; the skins of herbs are mostly applied to the body
surface of human body; the stems are mostly applied to the gastrointestinal tract of human body; the branches of herbs
are mostly applied to the limbs of human body; the seedlings and flowers of herbs are mostly applied to human body
surface; the fruits and seeds of herbs are mostly applied to the eye disease and reproductive system disease． Thus，the
rationality of the ancient Chinese people's medication theory，such as“the skin of herbs treats skin disease，the
herbs' section treats human joint disease，the herbs' kernals treat testicular disease，the herbs' seeds treat eye
disease，the herbs' vines treat tendons and vascular disease，and the herbs' branches treat limbs problems”，was
proven． The preliminary construction of its medicinal system is achieved．
Keywords therapeutic effects of herbs based on shapes; Chinese herbs; botanical drug; pharmacological method
state of traditional Chinese medicine; metaphorical cognition
