theoretical basis for the unity of nature and man． From the perspectives of five evolutive phases and six climatic fac-

movement in the middle，image forms outside，the constitution of body and disease tends，this paper explained the
theoretical basis and main carrier of the theory of unity of nature and man in traditional Chinese medicine． It is be-
lieved that qi transformation theory is the core of the theory of five evolutive phases and six climatic factors，which in-
