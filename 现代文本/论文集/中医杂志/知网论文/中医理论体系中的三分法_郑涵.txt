《黄帝内经》理论体系当中。通过梳理文献发现，一分为三的分类方法贯穿于中医理论体系中的各个环节，

On the Trichotomy in the Theoretical System of Traditional Chinese Medicine
ZHENG Han，LU Mingyuan
Shandong University of Traditional Chinese Medicine，Jinan，250355
ABSTＲACT The dichotomy and the quintomy，based on the yin yang，and five elements theory，are the most com-
monly used in traditional Chinese medicine ( TCM) theory，and the trichotomy method is also commonly found in the
theoretical system of Inner Canon of Yellow Emperor ( 《黄帝内经》) ． Through the review of the literature，it is found
that the classification method of one-three-dimension runs through all links in the theoretical system of TCM，not only
constructin the medical mode of“three dimensions”，but also forming the physical view of“three division of zang-fu
organs”，the etiology view of“three part qi”，the diagnosis view of“three body parts and nine pulse taking sites”，
and the treatment view of“three factors to different conditions”． Deeper understanding of the connotation and theoreti-
cal origin of the trichotomy can deepen the understanding of TCM theory．
Keywords trichotomy; yin yang theory; five elements theory; three creats all; medical mode of three dimensions
