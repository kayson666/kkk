FU Shan's Characteristic of“Heart-kidney Interdependence”Therapy
DU Caifeng1，WANG Tianfang2，WANG Ziwei1，ZHAO Yong3，LIANG Wenjing1
1． College of Basic Medical Sciences，Shanxi University of Chinese Medicine，Jinzhong，030619; 2． School of Traditional Chinese

ABSTＲACT “Heart-kidney interaction”and “water-fire harmonization”as important theories of traditional
Chinese medicine have became mature in the Ming and Qing Dynasties． When treating diseases，FU Shan lays empha-
sis on the relationship between the heart and kidney and puts forward the“mutual promotion within restraint”theory
meaning that “there is mutual promotion between heart and kidney though one restricts to the other”; with the idea of
“heart-kidney interdependence”，he advocates that the treatment of the heart can help to treat the kidney and vice
versa． Based on the“heart-kidney interdependence”theory，he develops many effective formulas in treating menstrual
disease，infertility，pregnancy disease and postpartum disease，men's fatigue and spermatorrhea，vomiting，insomnia
and many other diseases．
