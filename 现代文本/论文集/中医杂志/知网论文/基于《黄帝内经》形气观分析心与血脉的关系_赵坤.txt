［摘要］ 基于 《黄帝内经》形气观分析心与血脉的关系。认为心与血脉皆为解剖所见的实体结构，是天地
火气化生; 心为库藏，储藏太阳、火气，心与血脉的关系是心所储藏的太阳、火气对血脉具有化生与充养的
作用，即形与气的作用。《黄帝内经》时代的心主血脉没有心脏推动血液在经脉内运行的涵义，这对进一步
研究人体观及心藏象理论都有积极意义。

Analysis of Ｒelationship between Heart and Blood Vessels Based on Body-Qi Concept in Inner Canon of
Yellow Emporor (黄帝内经)
ZHAO Kun，LI Chengwei，WANG Qingguo
(School of Chinese Medicine，Beijing University of Chinese Medicine，Beijing，100029)
ABSTＲACT This article analyzes the relationship between the heart and blood vessels based on the body-Qi con-
cept in Inner Canon of Yellow Emporor (黄帝内经)． The authors regard the heart and blood vessels as the anatomical
entity structure，transformed and generated from the fire and Qi between heaven and earth． The heart is the storage of
Taiyang and fire Qi． The relationship between the heart and blood vessels is the transformation，the generation and
nutrition for blood vessels from Taiyang and fire Qi stored in the heart，which is the function of body and Qi． The
“heart governing blood vessels”in the Inner Canon of Yellow Emporor had no meaning that the heart could promote
the blood running in the channels and meridians，which is of great significance to the further study of the concept of
human body and the visceral manifestation theory of the heart．
Keywords Inner Canon of Yellow Emporor (黄帝内经); body-Qi concept; the heart governing blood vessels
