Research Thought of“Four in One”on Classic Prescriptions Based on Guizhi Decoction ( 桂枝汤)
MA Hongbo，PENG Min，MENG Hongyan，SI Guomin
Shandong Provincial Hospital Affiliated to Shandong University，Jinan，250021
ABSTRACT On the basis of classification research on classical prescriptions and syndromes，this paper expounds
research thought through summary and analysis on four elements of“property，position，tendency and syndrome”of
Guizhi Decoction ( 桂枝汤) ． Identifying the classical prescriptions from 4-dimensional perspective of“property，posi-
tion，tendency and syndrome”can make each classical prescription present in stereoscopic form，and facilitate us to
better understand ZHANG Zhongjing's thought of syndrome differentiation and treatment． Understanding the nature of
classical prescriptions，deeply analyzing its connotation，and striving to excavate its essence，have great significance
to correctly understand，learn，apply and study the classical prescriptions and improve the level of clinical diagnosis
and treatment．
Keywords Guizhi Decoction ( 桂枝汤) ; classical prescription research; formulaology
