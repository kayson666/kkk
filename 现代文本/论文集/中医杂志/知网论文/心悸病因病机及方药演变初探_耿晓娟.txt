GENG Xiaojuan1，2，ＲUAN Shiyi3，ZHANG Junping3
(1． Post-Doctoral Academic Ｒesearch Center，China Academy of Chinese Medicine Sciences，Beijing，100700; 2． School of Culture

ABSTＲACT The study on palpitation in traditional Chinese medicine ( TCM) started since Inner Canon of Yellow
Emperor ( 《黄帝内经》) ，and recorded in a lot of medical formulary and herb documents over the whole history of
TCM． This article revealed the development of the recognition on palpitation's etiology，pathogenesis and evolution of
application of related Chinese herbal medicine and formulas by teasing out the ancient documents of TCM，showing the
characteristics of medication in the treatment of palpitation along the history，and trying to provide some reference for
diagnosis and treatment of palpitation in modern clinical practice．
