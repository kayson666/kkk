Qi and Chinese Medicine View of Nature
SONG Zheng1，ZHENG Xiaohong2
(1． School of Humanities and Political Education，Nanjing University of Chinese Medicine，Nanjing，210023; 2． Nanjing University
of Chinese Medicine)
ABSTＲACT Chinese medicine view of nature is the overall understanding of nature and life formed in the practice
in Chinese medicine． Qi is the important concept of Chinese traditional philosophy and the theoretical cornerstone of
Chinese medicine，which has an important influence on the formation，connotation，nature and value of Chinese medi-
cine view of nature． Qi is the philosophical basis of the formation of Chinese medicine view of nature，and is the basic
connotation and core element of Chinese medicine view of nature． In Chinese medicine view of nature，Qi is the nou-
menon of nature． Meanwhile，Qi is the cognition method of natural epistemology，the evaluation criteria of natural val-
ue theory and the ethical carrier of natural ethics included in Chinese medicine view of nature． Qi determines the non-
structural nature of Chinese medicine view of nature，which is the characteristics and advantage of Chinese medicine．
Chinese medicine view of nature has important medical value，scientific value，ecological value and cultural value in
contemporary times．
